Alumni Signup Form

Greetings! The UNI Freethinkers and Inquirers are launching an exciting new program. Since the group’s founding in 2007, we have grown rapidly. The seven-person brunches that many of you remember now have 25 attendees each week. Our Darwin Week celebration is now recognized as one of the best secular student events in the country. With all of this growth, we are watching more and more members graduate and leave the group, and we don’t want to lose touch with them.

UNIFI's alumni will receive a semesterly update with news about what UNIFI is up to. We will also be soliciting blog posts from alumni who have remained active in the secular community about their experiences. In the years to come, we hope to have a yearly alumni event and more.

The second, and more important goal, is to connect current UNIFI members to you. As a group dedicated to science, reason, and secular values, we have already had countless alumni go on to pursue prestigious graduate degrees, land fascinating jobs, and continue to have an impact in their communities across the country. With your permission, we would like to offer you as a resource to current members who share your interests and aspirations.

If you’re interested in being a part of the alumni program, please fill out the form below. Your contact information will only be available with your permission and your other information will only be available to UNIFI members. Don’t hesitate to contact us with any questions, concerns, or suggestions. We are excited to see this program get started and look forward to being in touch with you in the future.
